What Are the STEM Nova Awards?

The Boy Scouts of America developed the STEM Nova awards program to excite and expand a sense of wonder in our Scouts. By working with an adult counselor or mentor, the various modules allow them to explore the basic principles of STEM and discover how fun and fascinating STEM can be. The Supernova awards are offered for those who enjoy a super challenge. Discover how the STEM Nova Awards program helps youth be “Prepared. For Life.”

Is This for Me?

Are you an Adult Leader interested in having more STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) activities in your Pack or Den? The Nova STEM Labs were created for you. Our goal is to make it easier for you to learn how to bring STEM into your unit and discover what kind of activities you can do in each area.

What Are Nova STEM Labs?

In Nova STEM Labs we break each Nova award down into a "Lab". A Lab is simply a defined journey for a Scout to go on that aligns to one of the BSA's Nova Award modules. Each Lab is designed to be completed in 4-weeks through a combination of Adult-led demonstrations, hands-on activities and at-home assignments. Each Lab will fullfil the requirements of the associated Nova Award module.

OK, Now What?

The easiest way to get a Nova STEM program going is to explain the Nova program at a unit meeting and gauge the interest level from the Scouts. The Nova Awards are designed to be achieved by Scout who show an interest in STEM activities - we don't recommend making it a requirment for all Scouts. The Nova STEM awards are definitely something the Scout should want to pursue.
