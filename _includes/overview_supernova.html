Supernova Awards

The most common Nova awards involve a group of requirements and activities in one of the STEM areas. A Scout completes the requirements and earns the Nova award. Completing the initial Nova award results in the Scout earning a patch for wear on the uniform, while each subsequent award earns the Scout a pin to be worn on the patch. Supernova awards require more in-depth and complex involvement. Earning a Supernova award involves earning several Nova awards plus completing other requirements. An adult mentor will guide and support a Scout working on the Supernova award requirements.
