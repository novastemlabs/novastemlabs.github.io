S is for 'Science'

The S in STEM is for SCIENCE. The Nova STEM Labs for science explore different kinds of science. In these labs you will learn about science in everyday life, earth science, aniomal science, scpace science, and achealogical science (the study of civilizations). You can start with any of the Nova STEM Labs you'd like.
