Nova STEM Labs

STEM—Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics

STEM is part of an initiative the Boy Scouts of America has taken on to encourage the natural curiosity of youth members and their sense of wonder about these fields through existing programs. From science experiements to robotics, Scouts can’t help but enjoy the wide range of STEM-related activities. To support this initiative, the BSA developed the STEM Nova Awards program so that youth members have fun and receive recognition for their efforts. Cub Scout Nova Awards may be earned by Wolves, Bears, Webelos, and Arrow Of Light Scouts.


Nova STEM Labs and Cub Scout Pack 571 are hosting a Nova STEM Day on May 5, 2019. This free event is a great opportunity to introduce Scouts to STEM through fun and engaging hands on activities, and for adult leaders to learn more about how to start a Nova program in their unit.
